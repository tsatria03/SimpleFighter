Added the /undomap and /redomap commands, with the short forms /ud and /ro, for taking back and reapplying map edits while building. Undo restores the map to how it was just before your last change, covering adding, editing, and removing a line as well as pasting a whole map over the current one, including a /rawdata paste, and you can undo and redo repeatedly to step back and forth through your recent edits. You can now build sensors in the builder menu.
